Shopping

Quirky, independent shopping in West Dorset

Bridport’s high-street has a strong and vibrant selection of independent shops and grocers. The famous streetmarket, on Wednesdays and Saturdays, offers a mix of antiques and bric-a-brac alongside food, crafts and everything else.
