A Dorset Original

The coolest boutique hotel in Dorset.

The Bull is an award-winning, luxury hotel in Bridport, West Dorset. It’s where you come to discover a perfect nook of south west England, while enjoying a relaxed, friendly hotel with bags of savvy style. The Bull has 19 individually furnished bedrooms, two restaurants, a cocktail bar, private dining, a courtyard garden and games room.
